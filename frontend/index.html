💡 Essential First-Aid Guides
🥵
Heat Stroke / Dehydration
Move the person to a cool area. Give ORS or salt-sugar water. Apply cool water to their skin. Do not give aspirin or fever medication.
🐍
Snake Bite First Aid
Keep the bitten limb still and below heart level. Wash with soap/water. Do NOT cut, suck poison, or apply ice. Rush to closest PHC for Anti-Snake Venom (ASV).
🔥
Burns / Scalds
Run cool (not cold) clean water over the burn for 10–15 minutes. Cover loosely with a clean cloth. Do not apply toothpaste, ghee, or butter.
💔
Heart Attack Signs
Crushing chest pain, sweating, shortness of breath, pain in left arm or jaw. Call 108. Keep patient calm, sitting up. Chew a 325mg aspirin if available.

💡 Health Guides & Wellness Tips

Small daily habits lead to long-term wellness.

🥗
Nutrition

Balanced Plate Diet

Ensure your daily meals contain rice/roti, lentils (dal), vegetables, and milk/curd. Balanced protein prevents fatigue and joint pains.

💧
Hydration

Daily Hydration Check

Drink 8–10 glasses (2.5L) of water daily. Clean, filtered water prevents stomach infections, kidney stones, and constant headaches.

🧼
Hygiene

20-Second Handwashing

Wash hands with soap before cooking, eating, and after toilets. This simple action cuts stomach infections and diarrhoea by 50%.

🛌
Habits

Sleep Recovery

Sleep 7–8 hours nightly. Good sleep helps the immune system fight virus infections and reduces high blood pressure risks.

🦟
Disease Prevention

Prevent Standing Water

Ensure no water accumulates in coolers or pots. Clear standing water weekly to prevent dengue and malaria mosquitoes.

🧘
Habits

Moderate Active Walk

Walk briskly for 30 minutes daily. Walking increases cardiorespiratory efficiency and decreases type-2 diabetes risk factors.
