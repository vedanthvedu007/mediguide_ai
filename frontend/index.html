💡 Health Tips & Guides
💧
Hydration
Drink Enough Water Daily
Drink 8 glasses (2 liters) of water every day. Water keeps your body cool, removes waste, and helps organs work properly. Add nimbu pani for electrolytes.
😴
Sleep
Get 7–8 Hours of Sleep
Sleep helps your body heal and fight germs. Sleep at the same time every night. Good sleep prevents diabetes, heart disease and stress.
🥗
Nutrition
Eat Fresh Vegetables & Fruits
Eat colorful vegetables and fruits every day. Try rice, dal, sabji and one fruit daily. Avoid processed and fried food as much as possible.
🚶
Exercise
Walk 30 Minutes Every Day
Walking is free medicine! 30 minutes daily reduces heart problems, controls blood sugar, and keeps your weight healthy.
🧼
Hygiene
Wash Hands Properly
Wash hands with soap for 20 seconds before eating, after toilet, and after coming from outside. This prevents 80% of common infections.
